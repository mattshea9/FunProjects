Prizes & Glory

Fight for the Jersey

Three coveted jerseys. One glorious day of cycling and bulk consumption. Honor your inner champion.

🟡
Yellow Jersey
Most Hot Dogs Consumed

Awarded to the rider who wolfed down the greatest number of $1.50 hot dogs across all six Costcos. Condiments encouraged. Dignity optional.

🟢
Green Jersey
Fastest Costco Consumed

A timed sprint competition at Costco #3. First rider to finish their Costco order and exit wins the points — and the green jersey. Speed eating is a sport.

🔵
Polka Dot Jersey
Most Calories Consumed

The King (or Queen) of the Mountains of Food. Track every calorie — from samples to rotisserie chickens — and claim your polka dot glory.
